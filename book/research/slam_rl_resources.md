# Key Resources for SLAM and Reinforcement Learning

This document compiles a list of high-quality resources for learning the theory behind Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) and Reinforcement Learning (RL).

## SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping)

### Books

1.  **"Probabilistic Robotics" by Sebastian Thrun, Wolfram Burgard, and Dieter Fox**: The foundational textbook on robotics, with several chapters dedicated to SLAM.
2.  **"State Estimation for Robotics" by Timothy D. Barfoot**: A more modern and in-depth mathematical treatment of the state estimation problems at the core of SLAM.

### Online Courses & Lectures

1.  **Cyrill Stachniss's SLAM Course (University of Freiburg)**: A comprehensive university course with video lectures and slides available on YouTube. Covers everything from fundamentals to advanced topics.
    - [YouTube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLgnQpQtFTOGQrZ4O5QzbIHgl3b1JHimN_)
2.  **SLAM Course by Claus Brenner**: Another excellent, classic set of lectures on SLAM.
    - [YouTube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL_H_z2c3x1gL-I3JXSd53O_3j1aLpT-O1)

### Papers

1.  **"Past, Present, and Future of Simultaneous Localization and Mapping: Toward the Robust-Perception Age" (Cesar Cadena et al., 2016)**: A great survey paper that provides a historical overview and discusses the major components of modern SLAM systems.
2.  **"ORB-SLAM2: an Open-Source SLAM System for Monocular, Stereo and RGB-D Cameras" (Ra√∫l Mur-Artal et al., 2017)**: The paper for one of the most famous and widely used open-source SLAM systems.

## Reinforcement Learning (RL)

### Books

1.  **"Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction" by Richard S. Sutton and Andrew G. Barto**: The definitive textbook for RL, covering the topic from the ground up.
    - [Online PDF](http.incompleteideas.net/book/the-book-2nd.html)

### Online Courses & Lectures

1.  **David Silver's Reinforcement Learning Course (DeepMind/UCL)**: A classic course that provides a deep dive into RL, from fundamentals to deep reinforcement learning.
    - [YouTube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLqYmG7hTraZDM-OYHWgPebj2MfCFzFobQ)
2.  **Spinning Up in Deep RL (OpenAI)**: An educational resource produced by OpenAI that includes a curated list of papers, tutorials, and code examples.
    - [Website](https://spinningup.openai.com/)

### Papers

1.  **"Playing Atari with Deep Reinforcement Learning" (Volodymyr Mnih et al., 2013)**: The seminal paper that introduced the Deep Q-Network (DQN) and kicked off the deep RL revolution.
2.  **"Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithms" (John Schulman et al., 2017)**: Introduces the PPO algorithm, which has become a standard and widely used RL algorithm.
